MyCoRe Office

If you have any questions about MyCoRe or if you need more information, please contact the MyCoRe office. The office advises interested parties, provides support for MyCoRe users, helps to find solutions with MyCoRe applications and coordinates the shared development of the system. The office is also doing editorial work for documentation und website. If you have specific questions about certain MyCoRe features, please contact the developer.
